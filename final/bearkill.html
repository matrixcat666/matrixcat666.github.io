You have chosen to use the Spiked Ball on the Zombie Bear!

The Spiked Ball is launched at the Zombie Bear. But it merely just gets caught in the sticky goop that is its melting flesh. Suddenly, you hear a disgusting guttural growl as the Bear turns to you.
