You have chosen to use the Chemical Dart on the Mutant Ant!

The Chemical Dart is launched at the Mutant Ant. It pierces the shell of its body and drains the fluid into it. But the liquid does not kill it- it only fills it with rage. The ant turns to you, and then charges at you, hissing and screeching. The last thing you remember is being toppled and giant pincers surrounding your head before they quickly close around you, crushing your skull like a walnut shell.

You have died.
