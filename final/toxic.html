You have chosen the Orange Mushroom!

The box tumbles out of the machine. You take the mushroom out of the box and eat it.

Suddenly you feel funny. Your vision and hearing becomes white and fuzzy. You start to feel dizzy. Then you see bright, vivid images of weird animals. Zebra striped tiger cats. Dancing dogs. Pink elephants on parade. A sparkling mongoose. Lots of rainbows. A magic portal to wonderland. So many happy images that make you feel happy. But you don't realize as your vision and consciousness slowly blacks out.

You have died.
