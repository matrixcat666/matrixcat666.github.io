You have chosen to use the Spiked Ball on the Mutant Ant!

The Spiked Ball is launched at the Mutant Ant. But it merely bounces off of its steel-like exoskeleton. The ant turns to you, and then charges at you, hissing and screeching. The last thing you remember is being toppled and giant pincers surrounding your head before they quickly close around you, crushing your skull like a walnut shell.

You have died.
